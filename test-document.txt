Crop Disease Management Guide

Introduction:
This document provides guidance on identifying and managing common crop diseases.

Common Crop Diseases:

1. Rice Blast Disease
Symptoms: Lesions on leaves, stems, and panicles
Management: Use resistant varieties, proper water management, fungicides

2. Wheat Rust
Symptoms: Rust-colored pustules on leaves
Management: Crop rotation, resistant varieties, timely fungicide application

3. Maize Smut
Symptoms: Swollen kernels covered in black spores
Management: Sanitation, crop rotation, resistant varieties

Best Practices:
- Regular monitoring of crops
- Early detection and intervention
- Integrated pest management approaches
- Proper sanitation and hygiene
- Use of certified seeds

Conclusion:
Effective disease management requires a combination of preventive measures and timely intervention.
